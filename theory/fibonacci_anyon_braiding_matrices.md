# Fibonacci Anyon Braiding Matrices (R-symbols)

In the Fibonacci modular tensor category, the braiding is encoded in R-symbols.

For two φ-anyons fusing to the vacuum or another φ:
- The R-matrix in the vacuum channel and φ channel differ.
- These matrices satisfy the hexagon identities together with the F-symbols (pentagon).
- Braiding two φ anyons generates a phase that depends on the fusion channel.

This is the explicit data that makes Fibonacci braiding universal for quantum computation. In the Codex, these matrices are the concrete operations performed when flux tubes are braided.
